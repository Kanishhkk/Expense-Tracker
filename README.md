## üìù Usage Guide

1.  **Add a Transaction:**
    *   Enter a description (e.g., "Groceries").
    *   Enter the amount.
    *   Select a category.
    *   Choose "Credit" (Income) or "Debit" (Expense).
    *   Click "Add Transaction".

2.  **Generate a Report:**
    *   Click the "Download Report" button in the top right.
    *   Select your desired report type: **Daily**, **Monthly**, or **Yearly**.
    *   Choose the specific date/month/year.
    *   Click "Generate PDF" to download your file.
